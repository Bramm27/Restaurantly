Gado-Gado
Gado-Gado is a popular Indonesian salad consisting of a mix of boiled vegetables, hard-boiled eggs, tofu, and tempeh, all drizzled with a rich peanut sauce. This vibrant dish is known for its balance of flavors, combining savory, sweet, and spicy elements. Often served with rice cakes, Gado-Gado is a nutritious and satisfying meal that showcases the freshness of local produce.
Bakso Malang
Bakso Malang is a beloved Indonesian meatball soup originating from Malang. It features tender beef meatballs served in a savory broth, accompanied by a variety of ingredients like tofu, egg noodles, and vegetables. Often garnished with fried shallots and served with chili sauce, Bakso Malang is a warm and hearty dish that showcases the rich flavors of Indonesian cuisine.
Aurora Ade
Aurora Ade is a refreshing beverage that combines a vibrant mix of fruits, typically featuring citrus elements like orange, lemon, and lime. Often sweetened with sugar or honey, this drink is known for its bright colors and invigorating flavors. Perfect for quenching thirst on hot days, Aurora Ade is a delightful choice for gatherings and celebrations, providing a burst of fruity goodness in every sip.
Klepon Cake
Klepon Cake is a delightful Indonesian dessert inspired by the traditional klepon, which are sweet rice cake balls filled with palm sugar and coated in grated coconut. This cake features a moist, pandan-flavored sponge base layered with a rich coconut cream frosting and sprinkled with grated coconut for added texture. The combination of sweet and fragrant flavors makes Klepon Cake a unique and irresistible treat.
Sinom
Sinom is a traditional Indonesian herbal drink made from young tamarind leaves, cinnamon, and palm sugar. It has a refreshing sweet and tangy flavor and is enjoyed both chilled and warm. Rich in antioxidants, Sinom offers potential health benefits, making it a delightful and nutritious beverage.
Grill Brisket beef Rawon Glaze Surabaya
Grill Brisket Beef Rawon Glaze is a fusion dish that combines the tender, smoky flavors of grilled beef brisket with a rich and aromatic rawon glaze, a traditional Indonesian black beef soup. This dish features perfectly cooked brisket marinated in a blend of spices, including keluak nuts, lemongrass, and ginger, providing a unique depth of flavor. Served with fragrant rice and fresh herbs, this culinary creation offers a delicious twist on classic Surabaya cuisine.
